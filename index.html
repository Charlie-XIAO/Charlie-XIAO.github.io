About Me

My name is 萧尧 (xiāo yáo). I am a senior majoring in Honors Mathematics and Computer Science at NYU Shanghai.

I was born in Shanghai and graduated from No.2 High School Affiliated to East China Normal University (华东师范大学第二附属中学, 2017-20). After that, I entered NYU Shanghai, where I am currently studying. During the Fall 2022 and Spring 2023 semesters, I studied away in New York at NYU Courant.

I am really interested in Mathematical Sciences, Computer Science, and programming. My research interest is mainly in computer networking and system design. As for music and art, I like playing the piano in my spare time. Currently, I aim to become a software engineer in the future. If you want to contact me, you may reach me at yx2436@nyu.edu.
