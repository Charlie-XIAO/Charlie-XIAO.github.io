Project information

  • Duration: Aug. 2022 - Oct. 2022.
  • Place: Shanghai, China & New York, U.S.A.
  • Programming language: HTML, PHP, (a little) JavaScript.

Overview

The initial motivation to build this website is to share daily life with my girlfriend who live in a different city. The photos of our daily lives will soon vanish in tons of our chat history, and it is complicated to backup or archive these photos every time. Then I said, well, I will make our own website to transfer and archive photos with comments. That was the initial version. As time passed and more functionalities were integrated into the website, it looked more like a simple social website than just a chat system. That is how the current version of this simple social website was developed.

Back to the point, this is a simple social website referencing Instagram and WeChat. Basic functionality include:

  • Account management.
  • Personalized settings.
  • Adding followers and creating posts (supporing text, images, and files).
  • Liking and commenting posts.
  • Notifications for new posts, comments, and likes.
