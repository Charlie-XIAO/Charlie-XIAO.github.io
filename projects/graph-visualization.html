Abstract

Most existing methods for graph visualization based on dimension reduction face performance issues that limit their applications to relatively small graphs. In this work, we propose a novel dimension reduction method for graph visualization, called t-Distributed Stochastic Graph Neighbor Embedding (t-SGNE). t-SGNE is specifically designed to visualize cluster structures of the graph. As a variant of the standard t-SNE method, t-SGNE avoids the time-consuming computations of pairwise similarity. Instead, it uses the neighbor structures of the graph effectively, reducing the time complexity and can be used in larger graphs. Codes and data will be available at Yao Xiao's GitHub repository.
