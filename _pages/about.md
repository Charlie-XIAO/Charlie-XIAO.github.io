My name is 萧尧 (xiāo yáo). I am a **Senior** majoring in **Honors Mathematics** and **Computer Science** at [**NYU Shanghai**](https://shanghai.nyu.edu/).

I was born in Shanghai and graduated [No.2 High School Affiliated to East China Normal University](http://www.hsefz.cn) (华东师范大学第二附属中学, 2017-20). After that, I entered [NYU Shanghai](https://shanghai.nyu.edu/), where I am currently studying. During the Fall 2022 and Spring 2023 semesters, I studied away in New York at [NYU Courant](https://cims.nyu.edu/dynamic/).

My research interest is mainly in network and system design. I am also actively involved in open source development, for which details can be viewed under the [projects page](/projects/). As for music and art, I like playing the piano in my spare time. Currently, I aim to become a software engineer in the future.
