My name is 萧尧 (xiāo yáo). I am a **Senior** majoring in **Honors Mathematics** and
**Computer Science**.

I was born in Shanghai and currently at [NYU Shanghai](https://shanghai.nyu.edu/)
(2020-) for undergraduate studies. During the Fall 2022 and Spring 2023 semesters, I
studied away in New York at [NYU Courant](https://cims.nyu.edu/dynamic/).

My research interest is mainly in networks and systems design. I am also actively
involved in open source software development, currently being a core developer of
[scikit-learn](https://scikit-learn.org/). More details regarding my research projects
and open source contributions can be found under the [projects page](/projects/). If
you want to contact me, you may reach me at [`yx2436@nyu.edu`](mailto:yx2436@nyu.edu).
