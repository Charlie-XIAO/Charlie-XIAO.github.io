This is a graduate course at the NYU Center for Data Science. Undergraduates are allowed to enroll in graduate level courses at NYU with permission of advisors and the Data Science Department. I received permission from Tina Lam, Assistant Director of Graduate Programs, NYU CDS.

Course information

  • Instructors: Mengye Ren and Ravid Shwartz-Ziv.
  • Semester: Spring 2023.
  • Outline: Machine learning and statistical modeling; Statistical learning theory, convex optimization, generative and discriminative models, kernel methods, boosting, latent variable models; etc.
  • Programming Language: Python.
